Welcome to Lakeview Park and Recreation.

Where you can relax, have fun and adventure.


Whether it's relaxing walks or wild water sports, Lakeview Park has it all. Set in 200 acers, there's forest, lakes, and trails. With cafes and restaurants and other amenities, you can spend the whole day here, and want to come back for more.
